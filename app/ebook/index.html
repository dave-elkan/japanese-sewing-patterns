Questions?

Should I buy the package or just the ebook?

The package is designed to give you everything to get started on understanding and sewing Japanese sewing patterns. It equips you with the ebook, as well as two sewing patterns to build up your confidence. The pattern instructions are illustrated with English translations and sewing tutorials to make it as easy as possible. If you are experienced at sewing Japanese patterns AND already have some Japanese sewing patterns on hand, just the ebook will give you the translations and guidance you need.

What sizes are available in the two sewing patterns?

The skirt and maxi dress sewing patterns come in the Japanese sizes S, M, L and LL. Please refer to the Sizing Chart to check the measurements. Both sewing patterns can be easily altered to bigger and smaller sizes.

I've never seen a Japanese sewing pattern. Is this ebook still relevant?

"How to Sew Japanese Sewing Patterns" is perfect to familiarise yourself with the wonder of Japanese sewing patterns. It's a great introduction to their structure, variety, where to find them and how to use them to make beautiful garments.
